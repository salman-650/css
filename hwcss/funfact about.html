🦁 Fun Facts About Lions: 1. A lion’s roar can be heard from up to 8 kilometers (5 miles) away! ➤ That’s why it’s called the King of the Jungle. 2. Female lions do most of the hunting. ➤ They work as a team to catch prey while the males guard the pride. 3. Lions can sleep up to 20 hours a day. ➤ Pretty lazy for a king, right? 4. Lions used to live in Europe too! ➤ Ancient Greek art and stories include lions. 5. A group of lions is called a “pride”. ➤ A pride usually has several females, their cubs, and a few males.
